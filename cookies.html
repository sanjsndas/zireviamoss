🍪 Cookies Policy

We use cookies like a gardener uses a trowel — lightly, purposefully, and always with care. Here’s everything you need to know.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our content, and make your experience smoother. Think of them as tiny bookmarks for your visit.

How we use cookies

We keep things minimal and transparent. Here’s the full list of cookies we use:

NamePurposeDuration
moss_prefRemembers your preferred theme or reading mode (if you choose one).1 year
moss_sessionKeeps you logged in (if we ever add accounts) — purely functional.Session
moss_analyticsAnonymized tracking of page visits to see which stories are most loved.30 days
moss_newsletterRemembers if you’ve already subscribed, so we don’t bug you twice.6 months

We do not use any third-party advertising or tracking cookies. No creepy retargeting, no sharing with data brokers.

🌱 Your cookie choices

You’re in control. You can:

  • Accept all cookies (the default — they’re all harmless and useful).
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings (though some parts of the site may feel a little less cozy).
  • Delete cookies anytime — they’ll grow back when you visit again, like moss after rain.

To manage your preferences, just adjust your browser settings. For more details, email us at privacy@zireviamoss.com.

Third-party cookies

We don’t use external analytics or ad networks. The only exception is if you click a link to an external site (like a book recommendation), that site may set its own cookies — but we have no control over that. Always check their policy.

Updates to this policy

If we ever change how we use cookies, we’ll update this page and note the date below. We’ll also mention it in our newsletter if it’s a significant change.
