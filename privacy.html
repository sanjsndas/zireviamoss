🔒 Privacy Policy

Your data is safe with us — just like a seed nestled in rich soil. We believe in transparency, care, and zero weirdness.

Information we collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily share with us — for example, when you subscribe to our newsletter, fill out the contact form, or leave a comment. This may include:

  • Name and email address — so we can reply to you and send you the mossy goodness you signed up for.
  • Message content — when you reach out with questions, stories, or seed requests.
  • Technical data — like browser type and IP address (anonymized), to help us improve the site experience.

How we use your information

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide the information you request.
  • Send you our weekly Moss Letters (if you subscribed). You can opt out anytime with one click.
  • Improve our content and understand which stories resonate most.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Ever.

🌱 Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

Just email us at privacy@zireviamoss.com and we’ll handle it within 48 hours.

Cookies & tracking

We use minimal cookies — only to remember your preferences and to understand which pages are most loved by our readers. We do not use third-party advertising trackers. For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

Data security

We treat your data like a rare heirloom seed: stored securely, handled with care, and never left out in the rain. Our site uses HTTPS, and we regularly review our security practices.

Changes to this policy

If we ever update this policy, we’ll post the new version here and update the date below. We’ll also let you know via our newsletter if it’s a material change.
