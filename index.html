    ✅ Seed starting checklist

    • Pre‑moisten mix 24h before sowing
    • Fill trays firmly, not packed
    • Plant at depth = 2× seed width
    • Bottom water with dilute seaweed
    • Provide 14‑16h light after germination

    Frequently asked questions

    When should I start tomatoes indoors?

    Start 6‑8 weeks before your last frost date. Use a heat mat for germination.

    How often should I water raised beds?

    Finger test: if the top 2″ are dry, water deeply. Twice a week in summer, less in spring.

    What is no‑dig gardening?

    A method that layers compost and organic matter on top of soil, building fertility without disturbing soil structure.
